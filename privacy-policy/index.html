Privacy Policy of Apatani Biisi Kheta

Effective Date: Date - April 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Apatani Biisi Kheta" application ("App"), published by the Apatani Baptist Association, Ziro ("Publisher", "we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Â 
We collect limited information to provide and improve the App:

  1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device (Not Collected by Us):

    • User Preferences: Theme, Font Size, Font Family, Keep Screen Awake setting.
    • Favorite Songs: A list of song numbers you mark as favorite.
    • This data remains entirely on your device within the App's local storage (shared_preferences). We do not collect, transmit, or have access to this data. It can typically be cleared by clearing the App's data in your device settings.

  2. Information Processed by Third-Party Services (When You Initiate Action):

    • WhatsApp: Launching WhatsApp via the Feedback screen uses the url_launcher package. Your interaction is governed by WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. We do not collect data from this interaction.
    • Sharing: Using the Share button uses the share_plus package to invoke your device's native sharing function. The data shared depends on the app you choose, governed by that app's privacy policy. We do not collect data from this interaction.

  3. Technical & Usage Information (Processed by Firebase): To help us fix bugs and understand how the App is used, we utilize Google's Firebase services (Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics). These services may automatically collect certain information, including but not limited to:

    • Crashlytics: Crash reports, including device state information (OS version, device model, orientation), unique application instance identifiers (pseudonymous), and stack traces related to crashes or errors within the App. This helps us diagnose and fix bugs.
    • Analytics: Usage data (such as screens visited, session duration, feature usage patterns), device identifiers (like mobile advertising IDs, depending on your device settings and consent), general geographic location (e.g., country), and device information (model, OS version). This helps us understand user engagement and improve app features. Data collected by Firebase Analytics is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy) and Firebase specific terms (https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

How We Use Information

  • Locally stored settings/favorites are used only for App functionality on your device.
  • Information from WhatsApp/Sharing is only used to initiate those actions.
  • Crashlytics data is used solely for diagnosing and fixing bugs and improving App stability.
  • Analytics data is used in an aggregated, anonymous or pseudonymous way to understand usage patterns and make informed decisions about improving the App's features and user experience.

Data Storage and Security

User preferences and favorites are stored only locally on your device. Data collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics is stored and processed by Google on their servers according to their security practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your locally stored preferences or favorites list.

Information collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics is processed by Google as described in their privacy policies. We access this data in an aggregated or anonymized form for the purposes described above (bug fixing, usage analysis). We do not share this raw data with other third parties, except as required by law or as described in Google's policies.

Interactions initiated by you with third-party apps (WhatsApp, apps via the Share function) are subject to the policies of those third parties.

User Choices and Control

  • You can clear locally stored preferences and favorites by clearing the App's data in your device's system settings.
  • You can often limit ad tracking and the collection of certain device identifiers used by analytics services through your device's main settings (e.g., under Google settings or Privacy settings on Android).

Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child may have provided information through Firebase, please contact us.
