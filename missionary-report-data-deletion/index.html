# Data Deletion Request

## Missionary Report App - Data Privacy

We respect your privacy and provide full control over your personal data.

### How Your Data is Stored

The Missionary Report App stores all data locally on your device using an SQLite database. No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud storage.

### How to Delete Your Data

You have two options to delete your data:

#### Option 1: Delete from Within the App
1. Open the Missionary Report App
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap on "Clear All Data"
4. Confirm the deletion

This will permanently remove all your reports, activities, and personal information from the app.

#### Option 2: Uninstall the App
Simply uninstalling the app from your device will automatically delete all associated data, as everything is stored locally on your device.

### Data Retention

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is retained after app uninstallation
- No backups are kept on external servers

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (viewable within the app)
- Delete your data at any time
- Export your data (PDF reports)
- Contact us with any privacy concerns
