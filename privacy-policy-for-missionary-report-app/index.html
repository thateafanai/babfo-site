Privacy Policy for Missionary Report App

BCM Missionary Monthly Report System - Privacy Policy
Last updated: August 2025
             
1. PURPOSE OF THIS APP
This application is exclusively designed for Baptist Church of Mizoram (BCM) missionaries to submit their monthly ministry reports digitally.
 
2. DATA COLLECTION
We collect only ministry-related information necessary for monthly reporting:\n'
  • Personal identification (name, position, location)\n'
  • Ministry activities and statistics\n'
  • Contact information for official correspondence
 
3. DATA STORAGE
Local Storage: All data is primarily stored on your device using a secure local database.
Data remains under your control and can be deleted anytime\n\n'
 
4. DATA USAGE
Your data is used solely for:
  • Generating monthly ministry reports
  • Tracking ministry activities and progress
  • Official BCM reporting requirements
 
5. DATA SHARING
We do NOT:
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
    Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Access your data without your consent
 
6. CLOUD SYNC
When signed in with Google:
  • Data syncs automatically when internet is available
  • Encrypted transmission to secure Firebase servers
  • Access limited to your account only
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time
 
7. YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
  • Access all your stored data
  • Export your reports in various formats
  • Delete your data permanently
  • Opt-out of cloud sync (use local storage only)
 
8. SECURITY
We protect your data through:
  • Device-level security (PIN/password/biometric)
  • No unauthorized access to your reports
 
9. GOOGLE SERVICES
When using Google Sign-In:
  • We use Google Firebase for authentication and cloud storage
  • Google’s privacy policy applies for authentication data: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can revoke Google access through your Google Account settings.
 
10. CONTACT
For privacy concerns or data requests:
  • Developer: Thatea Fana
  • Email: thateafanai@babfo.org
  • Purpose: BCM Missionary Reporting
 
By using this app, you consent to this privacy policy.
